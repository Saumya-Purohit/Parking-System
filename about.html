It has always been difficult for many vehicles to get a parking space during peak hours in places like hospitals, hotels, shopping centres, airports, universities, and exhibition and convention centres. According to surveys, up to 40% of all traffic is produced by vehicles looking for available parking spaces. That is a serious problem to address, and one of the finest options to at least lessen the traffic congestion brought on by the aforementioned issue is the Smart Parking System. This application provides details regarding the parking spaces in the lot that have sensors that can detect the presence of automobiles, including their occupancy status.
Tracking parking trends in a parking lot is the second section of this application. The analysis provides details on which parking spaces are most and least used, as well as at what hours of the day. When there are several parking spaces available, this information is useful for selecting one while bearing in mind the location's past. For instance, if there are multiple open slots, the driver will want to select the one with a lower occupancy rate because the slot with a higher occupancy rate may be sought after by many other drivers, and you don't want to waste time getting there.



Objectives



  • The main objective of the application is to provide the occupancy status (vacant/occupied) of the parking spaces and also to get information about the parking history data on any particular date.
  • It helps to park easier and uses reduced time to retrieve vehicle.
